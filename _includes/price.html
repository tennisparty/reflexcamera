Battery

The system runs on a 12v battery. This must be bought seperately. Any chemistry is suitable so you can use lead acid and lithium ion batteries. This gives great flexibility. Lithium-ion batteries are lighter, easier to maintain and better in colder temperatures but are more expensive. The battery can be linked to a solar panel if setup in a sunny spot.
The system run time is dependent on the amp hours of the battery. Some indicative figures are given below*

  • a 12v 5ah - lasts for c.1 night
  • a 12v 50ah - lasts for c.8 nights
  • a 12v 100ah - lasts for c.16 nights
  • a 12v 200ah - lasts for c.32 nights
*The actual run time will be dependent on the battery temperature and night length
