About

Camera features:

  • Ultra fast detection
  • Weatherproof
  • Simple deployment
  • Long battery life 20-25 days runtime

Benefits:

  • Save data storage and analysis time
  • Surveying trees - searching for important roosts and to prove presence /absence in a given tree
  • Finding out length of stay in trees
  • Monitoring tree mitigation/ exclusion and disturbance
  • Finding out numbers of bats during radiotracking or other work
  • Valuing woodlands by roosts
  • Movements of bats across a woodland (exploratory bechsteins)
  • Identifying bat behaviour outside of roosts
  • Possibly useful for other animals such as swifts, badgers, newts etc..
